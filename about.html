About ONDA

Marketing, brand creation & creator strategy under one roof.

ONDA specialises in helping brands enter, grow and stay relevant in the European market – with a strong focus on creator-led marketing and community-driven retail.

We started ONDA to bridge a gap we kept seeing: brands either had strong strategy but lacked execution, or great creative without a clear direction. Our work connects the two.

We operate as a lean, senior team with a network of collaborators across content production, design, development and retail. That lets us stay flexible while keeping quality and thinking at a high level.

ONDA’s role is to make sure your marketing efforts are coherent, grounded in reality and set up to scale. Sometimes that means rewriting your positioning. Sometimes it’s about designing your first creator program or planning a focused launch in one key city.

In every case, we care about clarity, transparency and partnership – working alongside your team rather than on the outside.

Our principles

How we approach every project

01

Clarity before complexity

We start by simplifying: what you offer, who it’s for, where you’re playing. Then we design the minimum effective system to move you forward.

02

Creators as partners

We prioritise sustainable relationships with creators – fair, transparent and mutually beneficial – because good collaborators are not a commodity.

03

Execution included

Strategy only matters if it gets implemented. We stay close to execution, from content production to campaign rollout.
