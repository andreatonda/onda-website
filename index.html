How we work

Strategic, lean and execution-focused

We blend senior-level thinking with a hands-on approach, working as an extension of your team rather than as an external vendor.

Clarity first. Every collaboration starts with understanding where you are today – markets, channels, internal resources, and brand perception – and where you want to go.

Then we design the path. We prioritise the initiatives that will create traction quickly, while building foundations that can scale: clear positioning, coherent messaging and measurable campaigns.

Execution included. ONDA is not just a deck. We stay involved to run campaigns, produce content, coordinate with influencers and support your launch or ongoing operations.

Our vision

Building brands that travel well

ONDA exists to help brands move confidently across markets, without losing what makes them unique.

We specialise in helping brands enter and scale within the European market, aligning global strategy with local nuance. That means understanding culture, regulations, consumer behaviour – and the real people and communities that shape trends.

By combining structured marketing capabilities with content, creator partnerships and retail experiences, we enable brands to build presence that feels both premium and grounded in reality.

Whether you’re launching a new label, expanding an existing brand or developing proprietary products, our goal is the same: to make your marketing both effective and sustainable.

We believe good marketing is additive – it respects time, attention and intelligence. That’s the standard we hold ourselves to in every project.
